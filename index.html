My best experiences of London

during my year at Imperial College

Presentation

Hi ! My name is Eliane and I come from France.

I am staying one year in London as an Erasmus student.

I try to make the most of my time in this wonterful city, and I visit as much as I can!

My London top:

  • London has a lot of beautiful buildings. Go to the monuments page to discover them!

  • Go to the Museums - it's free :)

  • Come to London with an empty stomach and enjoy British cuisine !

  • One of the best thinks I like is walking in Hyde Park. I do it every day to go to Imperial and I am always amazed by the beauty of nature. You can look at my photographs to understand why I enjoy it so much!
